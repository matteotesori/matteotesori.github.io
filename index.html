JUST ONE SECOND PLEASE...

Due to my academic background, I've designed my website by taking inspiration from the style that I use to write my personal notes. I do really love to write such notes in LaTeX, using the beautiful Metropolis dark theme (by Matthias Vogelgesang) of the beamer document class. As consequence, each page of my website highly resembles a slide of a typical presentation in a 16:9 aspect ratio. If you are using a mobile device, like a smartphone or a tablet, I suggest to you to rotate it by 90 degrees anticlockwise (or clockwise) in order to switch from the portrait display format to the landscape one. By doing this, you will be able to match the layout of my "slide-look-alike" website and to have a better sight of the contents. Take in mind that my website is desktop-native, meaning that the best device to visualize it is just a regular computer. If you still prefer to use a mobile device and you struggle to read the part written with small text, you can always zoom in without loosing resolution. In the near future I will update the source code in order to make it responsive for the mobile readers. For now, please, just rotate your phone and zoom in when is needed.

I like feedbacks and I consider myself as an open-minded person, driven by the love of learning from other people how to do new or better things. If you have a suggestion, even the most irrelevant, regarding how to improve my personal website, please feel free to send me an email with your comments/observations/ideas. I would really appreciate it and I will try to implement your ref‌inements in the best way I can do.
